BUZZ

Drop a YouTube link, hit play, then tap the buzzer. It listens through your mic and buzzes on every beat.

Load a track and hit play first.

📳 Tapping the buzzer itself fires a real native haptic (a Safari quirk via a hidden iOS switch — works on iOS 17.4–26.4, patched out in 26.5+). The automatic per-beat pulses while listening stay visual-only (shake + flash) since iOS doesn't let scripts fire haptics on their own.
